# 04 — Application Route Distribution

## Positioning Performance (MATURE, 40+ papers)

Dominant application. Covers static PPP (cm-level), kinematic PPP (dm-level), multi-frequency, and uncombined models.

## Time Transfer (ESTABLISHED, 8 papers)

Quad-frequency, single-frequency, dual-frequency time transfer. Sub-nanosecond accuracy reported. BDS-3 PPP-B2b enables UTC dissemination without internet.

## ZTD/PWV Retrieval (ESTABLISHED, 8 papers)

Water vapor monitoring for meteorology. Validated against radiosonde and ERA5. Ocean-based and land-based experiments.

## INS/Multi-Sensor Integration (GROWING, 7 papers)

PPP-B2b tightly/loosely coupled with IMU, LiDAR, MEMS. Factor graph and Kalman filter approaches. Urban environment focus.

## HAS/CLAS/RTS Comparison (GROWING, 7 papers)

B2b compared with Galileo HAS, QZSS CLAS/MADOCA, CNES/IGS RTS. MIXED_PRODUCTS category. Demonstrates B2b is competitive but not universally superior.

## LEO Orbit Determination (GROWING, 4 papers)

Real-time LEO satellite navigation enhanced by BDS-3 PPP-B2b. Regional and global coverage.

## Seismic/Geohazard (EMERGING, 2 papers)

Earthquake source description (2021 Maduo Mw 7.4) and shake table seismogeodesy.

## Toolbox/Decoder (ESTABLISHED, 3 papers)

Open-source decoders: NavDecoder (Python), GKit SSRDecoder (C/C++), SDR-based decoding.

## PPP-B2b-RTK (EMERGING SINGLETON, 1 paper)

Single-station SSR-based RTK augmentation. Not independently confirmed.

## Smartphone/Low-Cost (EMERGING, 2 papers)

Smartphone-grade PPP-B2b and low-cost GNSS devices for oceanic PWV.
