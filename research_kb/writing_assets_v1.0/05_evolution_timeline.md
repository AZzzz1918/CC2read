# 05 — PPP-B2b Research Evolution Timeline (2020–2025)

## Phase 1: System Validation (2020–2021)

BDS-3 completes constellation (June 2020), announces PPP-B2b service (July 2020). First independent assessments appear.

**Key papers:** Nie 2021 (first B2b orbit/clock assessment), Lu 2021 (SDR decoding), Tang Chenggan 2022 (correction generation pipeline).

**Key question:** Does PPP-B2b work?

## Phase 2: Comprehensive Evaluation (2022–2023)

Service matures. Research shifts from "does it work" to "how well, where, under what conditions."

**Key papers:** Yan Liu 2022 (most comprehensive evaluation, 8 IGS stations), Yangyuanxi 2022 (BDSBAS+B2b system principle), Peida Wu 2023 (multi-platform kinematic), multi-frequency papers.

**New entrants:** Galileo HAS (Borio GHASP 2023), QZSS CLAS evaluations appear as comparison baselines.

## Phase 3: Application Diversification (2024–2025)

PPP-B2b proven. Research diversifies into downstream applications, cross-system comparison, and operational tooling.

**Key developments:**
- Earthquake/seismic monitoring (Jianfei Zang 2024)
- Water vapor meteorology (Zhou Linghao 2025, Wang 2024)
- LEO satellite navigation
- Smartphone positioning
- Open-source toolboxes (NavDecoder, GKit)
- PPP-B2b-RTK proposed
- BDSet public dataset
- Multi-system fusion (B2b+HAS+MADOCA)

## Implication for Thesis

The evolution from validation → evaluation → application provides a natural narrative arc for the literature review chapter.
